About the Charter

The ONSNC Pilot Charter establishes a community-driven model called the Hybrid Rotational–Anchor Democratic Polity (HRADP). It blends traditional wisdom, participatory democracy, and blockchain-enabled transparency for villages, wards, and learning towns.

Technology Integration

Blockchain Ledger

Every decision, fund, and proposal is recorded on an open, verifiable ledger accessible to all citizens.

AI Civic Advisor

Provides fact-checking, bias detection, and ethical tradeoff insights — supporting fair governance.

Digital Twin Village

Simulates policy impacts (environmental, social, economic) before real-world implementation.

Hybrid Governance Council

Participatory, transparent decision-making using DAO and blockchain-enabled frameworks for communities.

DAO Village Council

Decentralized, community-driven governance with transparent, democratic decision-making.

  • Smart contract–based voting and decisions
  • Student, parent, teacher voting rights
  • Transparent fund allocation ledger
  • AI-assisted consensus validation
  • Reputation-based and quadratic voting

Community Participation

Empowers every member to co-govern through transparent digital tools and assemblies.

  • Real-time proposals and discussions
  • Monthly community voting sessions
  • AI moderation and fairness check
  • Publicly auditable blockchain logs

Transparency & Accountability

Every transaction and decision is visible on a shared ledger — fostering trust among all stakeholders.

  • Open data and blockchain-based reports
  • Public performance dashboard
  • Ethics and audit sub-council
  • Deliberation archive for research

Pilot Implementation

The HRADP model will be piloted in selected communities for 3 years, combining rotational councils, anchor elders, and AI-assisted transparency.

  • Population: 500–5000 individuals
  • Duration: 3 years (renewable)
  • Evaluation: Bi-annual community assemblies
