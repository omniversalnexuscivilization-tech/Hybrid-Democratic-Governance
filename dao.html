Overview

The DAO Village Council blends local wisdom with blockchain-backed governance to ensure fairness, transparency, and inclusivity in every decision.

Key features

Smart contract–based voting and decisions

Proposals are submitted digitally and executed via tamper-proof smart contracts. Results are instant, transparent, and enforce council rules automatically.

Student, parent, teacher voting rights

Clear voting rights for each group, configurable for equal or weighted voting through DAO rules.

Transparent fund allocation ledger

Every donation, grant, and expense is logged on a public ledger for trust and accountability.

AI-assisted consensus validation

AI reviews proposals for feasibility and fairness, highlighting conflicts or duplicates. Final decisions remain with the community.

Reputation-based and quadratic voting

Contributors earn reputation over time; quadratic voting ensures balanced outcomes by limiting dominance.

Governance model

  • Proposals: Any member can submit a proposal; offline sabha discussion + online DAO voting.
  • Voting: Smart contracts enforce rules; supports equal, weighted, reputation, and quadratic voting.
  • Funds: On-chain ledger records inflows/outflows; monthly reports auto-generated.
  • Audit: Open records, community review, and rotating audit committees for oversight.
